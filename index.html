About

Established in 2009. dr GEEKsters have proudly serviced thousands of home & business clients in the United States, and even Worldwide.

We have experience with practically every brand device out there; SONY, Microsoft, Apple, Samsung, Lenovo, Sony, IBM, Toshiba, Compaq, HP, DELL, Nintendo, Atari, SEGA, Kurio, Bose, JBL, Alienware, Falcon Northwest, MSI, Asus, Acer, and many more!

We are structured to accommodate local walk-in clients, along with national, and global shipments.

Repair Services Offered

dr GEEKsters are set above and beyond the other technology repair companies out there because we provide services for:

  • * Any type of Computer ( Laptops / Desktops / All-in-Ones / Custom Builds )
  • * Any Video Game Console ( Atari / NES / SEGA / xbox360 / PS3 / PSvita / PS4 / WiiU / XBOXone / & MUCH MORE!)
